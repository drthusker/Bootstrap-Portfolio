About Me

I grew up in Nebraska City Nebraska, GBR. At 18 I joined the Army and left for Fort Jackson South Carolina. After Basic I was sent to Agusta Georgia for AIT. Following AIT I was sent to Bad Kreuznach Germany. While in Germany I met my wife who was also in the Army and we were married in Palmyra Nebraska while on leave. We stayed in Germany another year and a half before returning to the states.

After the military we were deciding between Nebraska City or Phoenix (her home town), it really wasn't a choice we moved to Arizona. We lived in Tempe, Chandler and Mesa before finally settling in Gilbert, AZ. We have 4 grown childern and are becoming pretty happy with the empty nest life.
